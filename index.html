    About Us







    Ingelengele Project is a land surveying company based in Durban and East London, we are committed in helping our clients with survey and spatial requirements. We are able to respond to large and small scale projects and pride ourselves in providing a comprehensive service with high quality survey deliverable. The firm provides a multi-disciplined offering that includes engineering surveys and topographical surveys. All our projects are carefully programmed to be executed efficiently and with minimal duplication. Health and safety is taken seriously on all projects.
